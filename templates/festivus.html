What is Festivus?


Referenced on the show, it is a fictional secular holiday invented by George's father, Frank Costanza. Celebrated on December 23, it is intended as an alternative to the pressures and commercialism of the Christmas season. Some have described it as "an all-inclusive December gathering", involving a Festivus dinner, an unadorned aluminum Festivus pole, practices such as the "Airing of Grievances" and "Feats of Strength", and the labeling of easily explainable events as "Festivus miracles."
